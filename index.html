Objectives

Promoting holistic education, research and extension, skill development, and social welfare (agriculture, animal husbandry & service sectors) for community empowerment.

  • Research

  • Training

  • Sociological function

Research, Extension and Educational function

  • To establish & promote integrated educational activities
  • To impart modern education among the students;
  • To render accessory services & vocational training to the weaker sections of all communities and backward classes;
  • To run educational projects in reference to NEP, 2020;
  • To open coaching centres and vidyalayas according to the policies & programme of NCERT.

Training & Skill developmental function

  • To run multidisciplinary service projects
  • To arrange training for employment & income generation: Time to time different vocational training of students, unemployed & underemployed personnel would be arranged in different areas of service sectors, agriculture & allied sectors, different industrial & allied sectors for employment & income generation;
  • To address livelihood security of unemployed & underemployed personnel through integrated training & skill development
  • To demonstrate integrated agricultural practices; along with eco-friendly lifestyle management;
  • To demonstrate different technologies & developmental models, sustainable to economic development on application of integral humanism;

Sociological & Service function

  • To organise and run all social activities
  • To run and maintain human charitable activities, welfare trust & NPC (Non Profit Companies) U/S 8 Company Act, 1956 at different Villages and towns/cities
  • To provide researchers and extension workers or their Research organizations an additional platform to facilitate the utilization of their talent for welfare activities of the Academy & their institutions or organizations;
  • To render delivery of service to different govt. Pvt. Organizations or individual for providing unskilled, semi-skilled and highly skilled manpower for addressing livelihood security, employment and income generation at respective ends;
  • To have survey work to identify the researchable problems faced by the people; and to formulate developmental projects on coordinating different sectors (service, agricultural & allied sectors, industrial & allied sectors etc.);
  • To motivate members for publishing annual reports, books & booklets, pamphlets, popular articles, newsletters, periodical, research articles and newspaper.
